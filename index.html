A lush garden retreat

within a tropical paradise


As you step onto your private balcony, enjoy the tropical sunlight that washes over you and the soothing breeze that kisses your cheeks. Your holiday has just begun at Shangri-La Mactan. Reluctantly, you leave the luxury of your room, but only to explore the pleasures within the resort. Breakfast at Tides, the resort's interactive all day-dining outlet, is the first stop. The lure of the resort's Marine Sanctuary is not to be denied. A short walk along the beach front leads to an underwater adventure where you encounter diverse species of fish, clams and coral on a snorkelling tour. Refreshed and recharged, you are now ready to explore the Queen City of the South, as Cebu is also known.

Museums and churches highlight Cebu’s vibrant history as a Spanish colony. Between locations, you admire the sights, sounds and life all around. Lunch is the furthest thing on your mind but you are unable to resist a sampling of Cebu's renowned succulent mangoes. Back at Shangri-La Mactan, Cebu, you instinctively head for Chi, The Spa. Immersed in a traditional treatment based on the five elements to harmonize and optimize your body’s energy, you relax and drift off to sleep. Cowrie Cove is where you dine tonight. The al fresco seafood restaurant is the place to end your day.

Welcome to Shangri-La Mactan, Cebu. You have found a tropical paradise on earth.

Amenities

Dive into water sports

Beach


Our private beach cove and marine sanctuary is a delight for children and adults alike. It offers opportunities to simply relax or engage with the environment through diving programmes, fish feeding and other water activities. When you need to recharge, head to Breeze for light snacks and refreshing drinks.

Pool


The resort offers two swimming pools amid 13 hectares of landscaped gardens. The Main Wing pool is home to the Aquaplay area and an exhilarating water slide, making it a favourite spot for children. Guests can also enjoy refreshments and drinks from the poolside Buko Bar.
